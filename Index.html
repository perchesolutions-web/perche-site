Common Questions

Everything you’re
probably wondering

Does the AI actually sound like a real person?
Yes — and we test it extensively before you go live. We use the most natural-sounding voice AI available and tune the script until it sounds like a real receptionist. We run 10+ test calls before launch and you approve the voice before we go live.
What if a caller has a real emergency?
We build emergency escalation into every script. If a caller says anything indicating an emergency — no heat, flooding, gas smell, no power — the AI flags it as P1, creates an urgent job in your CRM, and can forward the call to a human if you want.
Do I have to change my current phone number?
No. In most cases we set up a new number that forwards from your existing one. Your existing number stays the same for customers. They never know anything changed on the backend.
What CRMs do you work with?
ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, FieldEdge, Service Fusion, and Google Calendar. If you don’t use any of these, we can set up a simple Google Sheets workflow. We work with what you have — we don’t make you switch software.
How long does setup take?
Most clients are fully live within 3–4 weeks of signing. Week 1 is discovery. Week 2 is our build phase. Week 3 is testing and your approval. Week 4 is soft launch. We’ve never had a client take longer than 5 weeks.
What if I already have a receptionist?
The AI handles overflow and after-hours — it doesn’t replace your people. Most clients keep their receptionist and let the AI handle calls when she’s on another line, at lunch, or after 5PM.
Is there a contract?
We ask for a 3-month minimum commitment so we have time to optimize and show you real results. After that, it’s month-to-month. We don’t lock you into a year-long contract because the results should be enough reason to stay.
What does the monthly ROI report look like?
A plain-English summary delivered by the 5th of each month: calls handled by AI, jobs booked automatically, estimated revenue from AI-booked jobs, admin hours saved, and one thing we’re optimizing next month. No fluff, no jargon.
